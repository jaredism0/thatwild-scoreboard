Frequently Asked Questions

How do I change team names?

Tap on the team name field to edit it. You can change it anytime during the game.

How do I add points to a team?

Use the orange +1, +2, or +3 buttons below each team's score to add points.

How do I subtract points if I made a mistake?

Use the red -1 button below each team's score to subtract a point.

How do I change periods?

Use the + and - buttons next to "Period" at the top of the screen to advance or go back periods.

How do I reset the game?

Use the "Reset Game" button to start a new game with scores at 0-0.

Where can I see the game history?

The game events log at the bottom of the screen shows all scoring and period changes with timestamps.

Privacy Policy

This app doesn't collect any personal data. All game data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted anywhere.
